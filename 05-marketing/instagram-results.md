# Instagram Results

## Background

Real numbers from our first week of posting across 3 accounts. All organic, $0 spent.

---

## Content type performance

| Type | Typical views | Non-followers | Skip rate | Loop rate |
|------|--------------|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| Share-CTA | 175-234 | 80-94% | 50-60% | 0.7-1.5x |
| Humor / relatable | 30-110 | 10-52% | 0% | 1.7-5x |
| Tips / educational | ~20 | ~50% | 0% | ~7x |
| Carousel | ~90 | ~40% | — | — |

---

## Patterns

| Type | Reach | Quality |
|------|-------|---------|
| Share-CTA | High (80%+ non-followers) | Medium |
| Humor/Tips | Low reach | Perfect (0% skip, 5-7x loop) |

**Best combo:** "Send to someone who..." + "Follow for more"

**Biggest miss:** 0 profile visits, 0 follows — because we forgot the "Follow for more" CTA.

---

## Key takeaways

- Share-CTAs get the most reach and most non-follower eyeballs
- Humor/tips get perfect engagement (0% skip, high loop) but don't reach new people
- Carousels without "Swipe ->" on every slide get 0 engagement
- 1,000+ followers generated 0 confirmed app installs — Instagram builds brand, not install base
- Always include "Follow for more" — without it, views don't convert to follows
