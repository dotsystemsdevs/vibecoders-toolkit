# Instagram Strategy

## Content types by account

| Account type | Focus | Format |
|-------------|-------|--------|
| App account | Share-CTA + humor related to niche | Short reels (3-6 sec) |
| Dev account | Build-in-public + relatable dev humor | Reels + carousels |

---

## Welcome DMs

Send a welcome DM to every new follower. Keep it short and include a tracked link.

DMs are your highest-converting channel. Every new follower should get a message within 24 hours.

### App account — single platform

**Goal:** Get them to download.

```
Hey! Thanks for the follow

[1-2 sentences about what your app does and why it's different]

[Dare/challenge to try it + social sharing angle]

[tracked link] — 100% free, no login
```

### App account — dual platform

**Goal:** Route users to the right platform. Use this if your IG audience has a mix of iPhone and Android users.

```
Hey, thanks for the follow!

[1 sentence about what your app does]

Are you on iPhone or Android?

iPhone -> [tracked iOS link]
Android -> [tracked Android link]

Both free, no login needed
```

**Why this matters:** If your app is Android-only but your followers are mostly iPhone users, a single Play Store link gets clicks but zero installs. Dual-platform DMs fix this completely. If you have an iOS app too, route iPhone users there instead of wasting the interaction.

### App account — testing phase

**Goal:** Get them to join testing.

```
Hey! Thanks for the follow

[Relatable question about the problem your app solves]

[What the app does, keep it casual]

We're testing it right now, join here if you want in:
[early access link]
```

### Dev account — multiple apps

**Goal:** Get them to your link tree and let them pick.

```
Hey! Thanks for the follow

[What you build — apps, tools, etc.]

Here's what I've got:

[App 1] (platform) -> [tracked link]
[App 2] (platform) -> [tracked link]

Let me know which ones you like
```

**Key:** Adding platform labels (iOS/Android) next to each link sets expectations and reduces dead clicks.

## DM performance vs other channels

| Channel | Clicks/link | Effort | Verdict |
|---------|------------|--------|---------|
| **DM** | Highest | Active | Best conversion per impression |
| **Linktree** | High | Passive | Best set-and-forget |
| **Bio** | Medium | Passive | OK, not great |
| **Story** | Low | Active | Not worth it |

DMs feel like a personal recommendation. Bio links feel like an ad. That's why DMs convert 2-3x better.
