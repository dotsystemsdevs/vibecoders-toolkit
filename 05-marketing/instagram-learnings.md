## 2. Reels

### Formula
```
Hook (0-1 sec) + Relatable content + "Send to someone who..." + "Follow for more"
```

### Rules
- 3-6 sec videos
- Every reel needs "Follow for more" CTA
- Share-CTA = reach, Humor = engagement

---

## 3. Carousels

### Formula
```
Strong cover + "Swipe ‚Üí" on every slide + CTA on last slide
```

---

## Mistakes to Avoid

- Posting carousel without swipe CTA
- Posting reel without follow CTA
- Expecting follows without asking
- Long videos (keep under 6 sec)
